Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

RidePilot AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the RidePilot AI mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password used to create your account.
  • Trip Data: Earnings, mileage, dates, pickup and dropoff addresses you enter or import.
  • Vehicle Information: Vehicle details and maintenance records you provide.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the RidePilot AI service
  • To calculate your True Net earnings and tax estimates
  • To power the AI Strategist analysis using your trip data
  • To sync your data securely across your devices
  • To send notifications you have enabled

AI Processing

RidePilot AI uses Claude AI (by Anthropic) to analyze your trip data and provide earnings insights. Your trip data is sent to Anthropic's API to generate these insights. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing. We do not sell your data to Anthropic or any third party.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • Anthropic Claude: AI analysis (anthropic.com)
  • Expo: Mobile app platform (expo.dev)

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with advertisers. Your trip data is used solely to provide the RidePilot AI service to you.

Your Rights

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Children's Privacy

RidePilot AI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
